About Us and Our Mission

At EMC Land nothing is as important than to ensure everyone I do business with can and does achieve their goals for land owning. My name is Christopher Ross and I operate this company with the same passion for helping others that I did as a cardiac nurse. I can assure you that in order to help a person in any manner you must first be willing to compromise and communicate to find a mutual path to achievement.


The reason I started this company was to help people reach a goal that I myself was able to gain through the very practices that I use today to help others. No matter what your goal is for land owning, I can assure that EMC Land wants you to see that goal become a reality. I will personally go to greater lengths that many others in this business will not do to help those in need. No task or ask of anyone will ever deter me from continuing to work to that mutually beneficial partnership and agreement.



This business like so many others allow people to have limitless options for who they choose to deal with. However, as in all aspects it is the person you do business with that you must be able to trust. I am grateful for my nursing career allowing me to develop that caring ability to understand how important that trust is.



I look forward to you giving me the opportunity to help in any way I can.
